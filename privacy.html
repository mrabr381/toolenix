Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

1. Introduction

At Toolenix, we believe privacy is a fundamental human right. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. Because of our unique technical architecture, our data practices are significantly more privacy-conscious than traditional cloud-based tool websites.

2. Client-Side Processing Guarantee

The core functionality of Toolenix operates via client-side processing. When you use tools such as our PDF Merger, Image Resizer, or JSON Formatter, the file manipulation happens entirely within your web browser using HTML5 and WebAssembly.

We do not upload, store, transmit, or retain your files on our servers. Your raw data never leaves your device. Once you close your browser tab, the data is wiped from your local memory.

3. Data We Do Collect

While we do not collect your files, we do collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to maintain and improve our website. This includes:

  • Basic analytics (e.g., page views, time on site, bounce rate).
  • Browser type and operating system identifiers.
  • Crash reports to help us fix bugs in our tool scripts.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies strictly necessary for the operation of the site, such as remembering your Dark Mode preference (`theme: 'dark'`). We do not use third-party tracking cookies for targeted advertising.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Page.
